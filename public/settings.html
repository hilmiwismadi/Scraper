📋 How to get your VPS JWT Token:

  2. Login to your account
  3. Open Browser DevTools (Press F12)
  4. Go to Application tab
  5. In the left sidebar, expand Local Storage
  6. Click on your domain
  7. Find authToken or token key
  8. Copy the token value and paste it above
